About Pete Hanna Center

The Pete Hanna Center is a state-of-the-art arena located in Birmingham, AL. Known for hosting thrilling sports games, unforgettable concerts, and a variety of community events, our venue features modern facilities, comfortable seating, and a vibrant atmosphere for all ages.

Whether you’re here to cheer on your favorite team or enjoy world-class entertainment, the Pete Hanna Center is the place to be!
